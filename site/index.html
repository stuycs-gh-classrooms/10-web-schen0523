The Eras Tour

The Eras Tour is Taylor Swift ‘s ongoing international tour that started on March 17th, 2024 and is set to end in late 2024 (assuming that no more dates are announced). The show goes through all of Taylor Swift’s eras, one at a time, with different outfits and set changes for each era. A total of 44 songs are sung on the tour, with some being mashups or cut short, and it is approximately 3.5 hours long, excluding openers. The tour is very popular, with tickets being sold out almost immediately, and many swifties watching the shows live from their own homes. There has also been a concert movie that was produced, which has been released in theaters and is set to be released on Disney Plus on March 15th. The order of the eras she goes through during the show is first Lover, then Fearless, evermore, reputation, Speak Now, Red, folklore, 1989, Surprise Songs, and lastly Midnights.
