1989

Taylor rises up from under the stage and first sings Style. After that, she sings Blank Space, Shake It Off, and Wildest Dreams. Lastly, she sings Bad Blood. Of course, you can’t forget the “You forgive, you forget, but you never let it go” chant. Then we move onto I would have to say the most nerve-racking past of the show for the fans, the Surprise Songs.
