About

The Eras Tour is an international tour that Taylor Swift had started on March 17, 2023, and is continuing until late 2024. It is a approximately 3.5 hour long show. The show goes through all of Taylor Swift's eras (albums), singing a few songs from each album. Below is the order of the Eras that she goes through during the show.

Lover

  1. Miss Americana and the Heartbreak Prince
  2. Cruel Summer
  3. The Man
  4. You Need to Calm Down
  5. Lover
  6. The Archer

Fearless

  1. Fearless
  2. You Belong With Me
  3. Love Story

evermore

  1. 'tis the damn season
  2. willow
  3. marjorie
  4. champagne problems
  5. tolerate it
Side Note: When Taylor played at shows where HAIM was an opener, 'tis the damn season was replaced with no body, no crime.
