About

The Eras Tour is an international tour that Taylor Swift had started on March 17, 2023, and is continuing until late 2024. It is a approximately 3.5 hour long show. The show goes through all of Taylor Swift's eras (albums), singing a few songs from each album. A total of 44 songs are sung on the tour, with some being mashups or cut short. Below is the order of the Eras that she goes through during the show.

Surprise Songs

During the Surprise Songs, Taylor plays two songs of her choice, one on guitar, one on piano.The songs can be chosen from her whole discography. A song cannot be replayed once it has already been played, unless it was on Midnights or if Taylor had messed up while playing it. Occasioanlly, she may also bring out surprsie guests to sing a song with her. Though at the start of the international part of the tour, all surprise songs were reset. Popular pairings include Death by a Thousand Cuts and Clean, and Getaway Car and Maroon. Taylor also uses this time to do announce things, including music videos and albums. As a result, this section is always very nervous for the fans. evermore Live from The Eras Tour
